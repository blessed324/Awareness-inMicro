The Scope of the Issue

Microplastics are found everywhere, from the Arctic to the deep seas. They are ingested by humans and animals, leading to health risks and ecological imbalances.

Key Concerns

  1. Microplastics in drinking water and food items.
  2. Harmful effects on marine and terrestrial life.
  3. Accumulation of plastics in the food chain (bioaccumulation).
