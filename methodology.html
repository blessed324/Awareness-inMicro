Data Collection

We employ a mix of methods to collect data:

  • Field surveys to identify microplastic hotspots.
  • Lab analysis of contamination levels in food items.
  • Collaboration with researchers and organizations for shared insights.

Data Analysis

Using advanced statistical tools and machine learning algorithms, we uncover patterns between microplastic contamination sources and their impact on biodiversity.
